## Objection Handling

### Why not just use ChatGPT?
ChatGPT can write copy. OptivexIQ is built to diagnose the underlying messaging problem, prioritize what matters, and support a repeatable review workflow.

### Is this just another AI wrapper?
No. The product is structured around reports, prioritization, competitive context, and rewrite evaluation, not one-off prompt outputs.

### Will this actually improve conversion?
It is decision-support software, not a guarantee engine. Its job is to improve the quality and speed of conversion decisions before teams test and ship changes.

### Who is this really for?
Teams that already care about positioning and conversion, but lack a structured way to inspect buyer-facing narrative risk.

## Copy Style Guide

### Do
+ Lead with business clarity, not AI novelty.
+ Use `conversion intelligence platform` as the parent category.
+ Use `messaging diagnostics` as the entry wedge.
+ Emphasize reports, prioritization, evidence, and review workflow.
+ Frame outputs as directional guidance and decision support.
+ Write for skeptical SaaS buyers who dismiss generic claims quickly.

### Don't
+ Do not lead with `AI copywriting`, `AI audit`, or `optimization platform`.
+ Do not imply guaranteed lift, guaranteed revenue impact, or deterministic outcomes.
+ Do not overuse abstract phrases like `unlock growth`, `supercharge conversion`, or `transform your funnel`.
+ Do not present rewrites as autonomous publishing or automatic website improvement.
+ Do not let the product read like a prompt wrapper.
